About Raise The Paddle Counter

Raise The Paddle Counter is a simple and efficient tool designed to help fundraising events track donations in real-time. It allows event organizers to quickly tally contributions at various donation levels, providing an engaging and dynamic experience for both organizers and donors.

Features:

  • Multiple donation levels
  • Real-time grand total calculation
  • Easy-to-use interface
  • Customizable donation levels
  • Event management capabilities

This app was created to streamline the fundraising process and maximize the impact of charitable events. We hope it serves you well in your fundraising endeavors!
